1. Graph the solution set of the following linear inequalities in xy-plane:

    • $$ 2x + y \leq 6 \rightarrow (1) $$
    • Associated equation of (1) is,
    • $$ 2x + y = 6 \rightarrow (2) $$
    • For y = 0, (2) becomes, 2x = 6 ⇒ x = 3
    • For x = 0, (2) becomes, y = 6
    • Line (2) cuts x-axis at (3, 0) and y-axis at (0, 6)
    • Putting (0, 0) in L.H.S. of (1),
    • 2(0) + (0) = 0 < 6 (∴ (1) is satisfied by (0, 0))
    • So graph of (1) is the closed half plane on the side of (0, 0), or closed half plane containing (0, 0). The shaded portion is shown below:
    • $$ 3x + 7y \geq 21 \rightarrow (1) $$
    • Associated equation of (1) is,
    • $$ 3x + 7y = 21 \rightarrow (2) $$
    • For y = 0, (2) becomes, 3x = 21 ⇒ x = 7
    • For x = 0, (2) becomes, 7y = 21 ⇒ y = 3
    • Line (2) cuts x-axis at (7, 0) and y-axis at (0, 3)
    • Putting (0, 0) in L.H.S. of (1),
    • 3(0) + 7(0) = 0 < 21 (∴ (1) is not satisfied by (0, 0))
    • So graph of (1) is the closed half plane (made by line (2)) which does not contain (0, 0). The shaded portion is shown below:
